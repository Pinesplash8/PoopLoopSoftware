A New Exchange Student

Spade and Elisa meet for the first time a few minutes before class. They both wear necklaces and carry bags. Elisa, a new exchange student, wears a cross and headphones. The classroom is symbolized by a few chairs, a board, and a table. On the table sits a bone sculpture, and above it, four portraits of students hang high on the wall.

Elisa (peeping through the door):

Hi, is this George’s class?

Spade:

Yeah, hi. Are you on exchange?

Elisa:

Yes...

Spade:

Cool! Welcome, I'm Spade.

Elisa:

What?

Spade:

My name. Spade.

Elisa:

Spade?

Spade:

Yeah, like the card. The one that’s a leaf.

Elisa:

Ah, cool.

Spade:

And you?

Elisa:

Elisa.

Spade:

Where are you from?

Elisa:

Basel.

Spade (giggling):

Are you Catholic?

Elisa (defensive):

No! Why would you assume that? Because I'm wearing this? How is that related?

Spade (smiling):

Well, you also have that Catholic school skirt vibe.

Elisa:

Ugh! People just assume things. They just make assumptions!
